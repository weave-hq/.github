# Loom

**Loom** is an AI-native operations platform designed for real-world IT environments — networks, security, cloud, and infrastructure across multiple vendors and teams.

It doesn’t replace operators.  
It removes the fragmentation around them.

Loom coordinates systems, tools, and reasoning into **structured execution**, so complex operational work can be handled faster, more consistently, and at scale.

---

## The Problem

Modern IT operations are fragmented by design:
- Tools don’t talk to each other  
- Knowledge is tribal and scattered  
- Multi-vendor environments create blind spots  
- Troubleshooting is manual, slow, and inconsistent  

Operators spend more time stitching context together than actually solving problems.

---

## What Loom Does

Loom turns operations into **coordinated, executable workflows**.

It can:
- Investigate incidents across systems (e.g. NetBox, Cisco, VMware)  
- Correlate data from multiple sources  
- Apply structured reasoning to narrow down root causes  
- Execute actions or propose next steps with full context  

All while keeping the human in control.

---

## How Loom Works

Loom is built on a small set of composable primitives:

- **Loom Control**  
  Orchestrates execution, state, and lifecycle of work  

- **Programs**  
  Specialized reasoning units (investigation, research, execution)  

- **Cycles**  
  Structured executions of work — tracked, resumable processes  

- **Integrations (Weaves)**  
  Connected systems exposed as capabilities  

---

## The Clever Bit

Loom is **capability-driven**, not tool-driven.

Instead of selecting tools directly, Loom:
1. Determines *what needs to be done* (capability)  
2. Selects *how to do it* (best integration or combination)  
3. Executes and merges results across providers  

This enables:
- Multi-vendor execution without hardcoding  
- Parallel data gathering and correlation  
- Deterministic, inspectable behavior (not black-box agents)  

---

## Why Loom Exists

We don’t believe in replacing operators.

We believe in:
- **Determinism over magic**  
- **Coordination over autonomy**  
- **Systems that scale humans, not bypass them**  

Like the industrial loom increased the output of skilled workers,  
**Loom increases the reach and effectiveness of IT operators.**
